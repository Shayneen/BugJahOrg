About Us

Bug Jah Org is an Organization, founded in the St. Augustine Classroom. The Organization was also founded by Leinold "BugJah8" Francisco, with founding members such as Earl Matthew "JAHBINDIA" Hipolito, and Muhammad Rizwan "Jahwatre" Amilhamja.

The Organization believes in the full connection and collaboration within its members, upholding all values of a Bug Jah in the meantime, and also fulfilling the duty as a Bug Jah, such as doing tasks, accomplishing roles, and more.
